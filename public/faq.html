Got Questions? We Got Answers.

What is xTeraPlay?
xTeraPlay is a premium web application designed to extract and play video content directly from TeraBox links in a clean, fast, and responsive interface.
Is xTeraPlay free to use?
Yes, xTeraPlay is completely free. We monetize through non-intrusive advertisements to keep the service running for everyone.
Why is it taking so long to extract?
Extraction can take up to 60 seconds depending on the file size and the response time of the source servers. Please be patient while our engine works its magic.
Does it work on mobile?
Absolutely! xTeraPlay is built with a mobile-first approach and is fully responsive on all devices, including tablets and smartphones.
